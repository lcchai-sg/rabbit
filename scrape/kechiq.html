Questions and answers
How long do shipping and delivery take?
Estimated delivery times vary based on the product, the seller’s availability, shipment type and destination. Estimated delivery times are listed on the product’s information sheet in the order confirmation email and also appear in your reserved area in the ORDERS section after placing the order. Before placing your order, you will be able to view the estimated final delivery date based on the destination address entered during checkout.
Is shipping free of charge?
Yes, standard shipping is always free of charge for all destinations.
How can i place an order?
Buying is easy! Just select the watch you wish to buy, enter the product page, click on BUY NOW and then on GO TO CHECKOUT. From here, click on CONFIRM ORDER. You will have to enter and confirm the shipping address, the preferred payment method and accept our Terms and Conditions. Then, click on CONFIRM ORDER again to complete the purchase.
How to request gift wrapping?
Kechiq offers you the possibility to request gift wrapping by choosing from the different options available. Gift wrapping consists of themed wrapping paper, a decorative ribbon and a courtesy invoice with a personalised greeting message. In the ORDER SUMMARY, before making the payment, you can choose whether to receive the product already gift-wrapped or include the gift paper and ribbon kit separately so you can wrap it yourself.
Are the watches genuine?
All the watches on the Kechiq website are new and authentic: each watch comes with its original packaging, a multilingual instruction booklet and a 2-year warranty.

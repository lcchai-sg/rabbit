Swiss Made Watches

Longines is proud to include the Swiss Made mark on all of its watches. This simple wording guarantees a standard of quality that has been established over many centuries and is today a world-renowned symbol of excellence.

On top of Longines own proven excellence, the Swiss Made mark provides customers with even more assurance and trust in the watch they wear on their wrist.

Free Shipping & 30-Day Returns

Delivery:

We offer free shipping on all orders placed on longines.com/en-us. Place your order before 12PM ET for same day processing Monday through Friday. Watches requiring bracelet sizing may take additional time to process.

Returns:

Customer satisfaction is our goal. If you are not satisfied with your product, you may safely return any unworn, undamaged merchandise purchased from longines.com/en-us within 30 days of receipt for a full refund of the purchase price.

Proceed with order

The Montblanc Star Legacy product line takes its inspiration from Minervas heritage and the companys pocket watches, made during the late 19th and early 20th centuries. The Montblanc Star Legacy Chronograph Day & Date offers useful complications and classical watchmaking along with a stylish, yet casual look. It comes in a 43 mm stainless steel case and features an ivory-colored dial with 18 K rose gold-coated Roman numerals, indexes and luminescent hands for the time functions and blued hands for the chronograph readings. The timepiece is equipped with the MB 25.07 chronograph movement that indicates the elapsed seconds via a central chronograph seconds hand, the elapsed minutes in a 30-minute counter, and the elapsed hours in a 12-hour counter. The day and date displays can be found in apertures at 3 oclock.

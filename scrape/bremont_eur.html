ALT1-WT

0% APR Interest Finance Available

The Bremont ALT1-WT World Timer is a wonderfully over-engineered timepiece and is one of the more intricate military watches ever produced. Created for pilots and travellers the world over, it incorporates a complex, highly-finished dial. The Roto-Click bezel enables the user to work out global time zones using the International landing location. And the adjustable 24-hour hand can display UTC or Zulu time.
